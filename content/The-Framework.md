# The Framework

---

Everything Ordyth produces is built on something.

A framework. A structured system for understanding how coherent living actually works — not as a philosophy to admire, but as a set of precise mechanisms to apply.

That framework is called **Praxium**.

*The Human Coherence Framework.*

It maps the gap between who a person is and how they live — why that gap forms, how it persists, and what it actually takes to close it. Permanently, not temporarily.

Praxium is currently in development.

It is not yet ready to be released in full — because releasing a framework before it is complete would be exactly the kind of borrowed standard Ordyth exists to help people move away from.

**It will be released when it is ready. Not before.**

What exists now is its first expression — the Apollo Series of books, the Aware · Align · Anchor operating framework, and the content and thinking of Ordyth itself. Every piece of it is Praxium in practice, even before the framework is named in full.

*When it is ready — you will find it here.*
